About

Welcome to the 3D Printing and Design Club, where creativity takes shape! We are a vibrant community of students united by our passion for exploring the limitless possibilities of 3D printing and design. From beginners to experts, our diverse membership shares a common goal: to learn, collaborate, and innovate in the dynamic world of additive manufacturing.Through workshops, design challenges, guest speakers, and hands-on projects, we strive to empower our members to turn their visions into tangible creations. Join us and become part of a community that's shaping the future of manufacturing and design. Together, let's build, innovate, and transform ideas into reality with 3D printing.

Vision

Our vision is to cultivate a culture of creativity, innovation, and excellence within the 3D Printing and Design Club, where every member is empowered to explore, experiment, and excel in the dynamic realm of additive manufacturing. We aspire to be at the forefront of shaping the future of design and manufacturing, driving forward-thinking solutions and inspiring positive change through our collective passion for 3D printing technology.

Mission

Our mission is to provide a supportive platform for students to learn, experiment, and innovate with 3D printing technology. We aim to foster a community of learners and creators who share knowledge, exchange ideas, and push the boundaries of design possibilities. Through workshops, projects, and networking opportunities, we strive to equip our members with the skills and confidence to make a meaningful impact in the world of 3D printing and design.

FAQs

What are the software will be covered in the club?

We will cover some popular design software and 3D printing software such as Fusion 360, Catia V5, Creo, Ultimaker Cura.

How can I register for the worksop?

Registration can be done through by clicking the register button in the event page and event details ar send through the community before 3-5 days the workshop.

Will there be hands-on training during the workshop

Yes, the participants will have the opportuniyu for hands-on practice with the software with your own laptops.

Are there any prerequisties or recommended skills for participants?

No, prerequisties are required, but familiarity with the basic knowledge about CAD and technical knowledge is beneficial.

Who will be leading the workshop?

All the event which are conducted by this club which are conducted only by the student under the guidance of a mentor.
